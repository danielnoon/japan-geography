Japanese Geography and Culture

Daniel Noon

The Islands　島

Hokkaido 北海道

  • “North Sea Way”
  • Northernmost island
  • 10% of land is national parks
  • Cold, snowy winters

Honshu 本州

  • 7th largest island in the world
  • “Main State”
  • Home of the world's largest metropolitan area—Tokyo
  • Warm summers and cool winters

Kyushu 九州

  • “Nine Provinces”
  • Southernmost island
  • Subtropic climate
  • Hot summers

Shikoku 四国

  • “Fourth Country”
  • Southwest of Honshu
  • Smallest and least populous island

Geography and Climate　地理と気候

Physical Geography

  • Mountainous, many dialects
  • Rivers
  • No one is farther than 70 miles from the sea, fishing
  • Flat plains
    • Kanto
    • Rice farming

Climate Variety

  • Hokkaido north
    • Cold Winters
    • Snow (lots)
  • Shikoku and Kyushu south
    • Warm beaches
    • Subtropic
    • Hot summers
  • Tokyo
    • Summers similar to Omaha
    • Ocean moderation
  • Typhoons

Seismic Activity

  • In the Ring of Fire
  • Earthquakes common
  • Tsunami
  • Disasters very common, which builds resiliency

Volcanism

  • Hot springs and onsen
  • Volcanoes
    • Mt. Fuji
    • Mt. Tate
    • Mt. Haku
