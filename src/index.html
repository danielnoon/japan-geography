The Islands

Hokkaido 北海道

  • “North Sea Way”
  • Northernmost island
  • 10% of land is national parks
  • Cold, snowy winters

Honshu 本州

  • 7th largest island in the world
  • “Main State”
  • Home of the world's largest metropolitan area—Tokyo
  • Warm summers and cool winters

Kyushu 九州

  • “Nine Provinces”
  • Southernmost island
  • Subtropic climate
  • Hot summers

Shikoku 四国

  • “Fourth Country”
  • Southwest of Honshu
  • Smallest and least populous island
